I am a Ph.D. candidate in theoretical chemistry at the department of chemistry, Johns Hopkins University. My research interests span quantum chemistry and quantum computing. I am experienced in coupled-cluster theory based method development and applications in chemistry. I am advised by Prof. Lan Cheng.

Previously, I completed my bachelors and masters dual degree at IIT Kanpur in India. I worked on my masters thesis in the lab of Prof. Debashis Mukherjee on extended wick's theorem and its applications in multi-reference quantum chemistry methods.
